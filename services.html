Shipping times depend on your service plan:

  • Basic Plan: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days.
  • Standard Plan: Express shipping delivers within 2-3 business days.
  • Premium Plan: Priority shipping arrives within 24-48 hours.

All orders include real-time tracking so you can monitor your delivery every step of the way. Free shipping is available on all orders over $100.

Yes! We ship to 45+ countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination. Premium plan members enjoy expedited international shipping within 5-7 business days.

Import duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations. We provide all necessary customs documentation to ensure smooth delivery.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with your tracking number and a direct link to track your package in real-time.

You can also track your order by:

  1. Logging into your TechWave account
  2. Going to "My Orders" section
  3. Clicking the tracking number for live updates

Our tracking system provides GPS-level accuracy with estimated delivery windows.

We offer a hassle-free return policy based on your service plan:

  • Basic: 3-day exchange window
  • Standard: 7-day exchange with free return shipping
  • Premium: 14-day exchange with free return shipping and full refund option

Items must be returned in their original packaging with all accessories included. Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.

Initiating a return is simple:

  1. Log into your TechWave account
  2. Navigate to "My Orders"
  3. Select the item you want to return
  4. Choose "Return" or "Exchange" and select a reason
  5. Print the prepaid return label
  6. Drop off at your nearest shipping partner location

Alternatively, you can contact our 24/7 support team and they'll arrange a free pickup from your doorstep.

Yes, you can still return opened products within the exchange window of your plan. We understand you need to open and test the product. The product should be in working condition with all original accessories.

Note: Products with physical damage caused by misuse are not eligible for return but may be covered under your warranty plan.

Our comprehensive warranty covers:

  • Manufacturing defects — any defect in materials or workmanship
  • Component failures — internal hardware or electronic failures
  • Software issues — firmware bugs or software malfunctions
  • Battery degradation — below 80% capacity within warranty period

The warranty does not cover damage from accidents, misuse, unauthorized modifications, or natural disasters.

To claim warranty service:

  1. Contact our support team via phone, chat, or email
  2. Provide your order number and describe the issue
  3. Our team will run remote diagnostics if possible
  4. If repair is needed, we'll arrange free pickup
  5. Repairs are typically completed within 5-10 business days
  6. If unrepairable, we'll provide a replacement

Premium plan members get priority repair service with a loaner device while theirs is being repaired.

Absolutely! You can extend your warranty at any time before the current warranty expires:

  • +2 Years Extension: $29.99
  • +5 Years Extension: $59.99
  • Lifetime Protection: $99.99 (covers the entire product lifespan)

Warranty extensions can be purchased through your account settings or by contacting our support team. Upgrading your service plan also automatically extends your warranty.

We accept a wide range of payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay
  • Buy Now Pay Later: Afterpay, Klarna, Affirm (0% interest for 6 months)
  • Bank Transfer: Direct ACH transfer
  • Cryptocurrency: Bitcoin, Ethereum (via Coinbase Commerce)

All payments are processed through our 256-bit SSL encrypted and PCI-DSS compliant gateway.

Yes! We offer flexible installment plans for purchases over $100:

  • 3-Month Plan: 0% interest, split into 3 equal payments
  • 6-Month Plan: 0% interest, split into 6 equal payments
  • 12-Month Plan: Low 4.9% APR, split into 12 equal payments

Installment plans are available through Afterpay, Klarna, and Affirm. Simply select your preferred option at checkout. No credit score impact for checking eligibility.

You can apply discount codes at checkout:

  1. Add items to your cart
  2. Proceed to checkout
  3. Enter your promo code in the "Discount Code" field
  4. Click "Apply" to see your savings

Important notes:

  • Only one discount code can be used per order
  • Loyalty rewards points can be combined with discount codes
  • Sale items may have limited promotional eligibility
  • Subscribe to our newsletter for exclusive discount codes!

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  1. Click the Profile icon in the top navigation bar
  2. Click "Sign up" to switch to the registration form
  3. Enter your name, email, and create a password
  4. Click "Sign Up" to create your account

You can also sign up during checkout. Your account gives you access to order tracking, wishlist, loyalty rewards, and personalized recommendations.

Upgrading your service plan is simple:

  1. Go to the Service Comparison section on this page
  2. Review the features of each plan
  3. Click "Upgrade" on your preferred plan
  4. Complete the payment process

When you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be credited for the remaining time on your current plan. The upgrade takes effect immediately and applies to all your existing and future orders.

How points work:
